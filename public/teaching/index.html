  • GIS for territorial diagnosis (French) Module taught at the Mediterranean Agronomic Institute of Montpellier (CIHEAM-IAMM) to the Masters programs I3P and GAT on how to use GIS for territorial diagnosis. It introduces a systematic methodology for the analysis of specific spatial units, such as functional urban areas, aimed at identifying key challenges and opportunities as a basis for strategic investment decisions for a certain area, city or region, in line with a place-based and integrated approach.
