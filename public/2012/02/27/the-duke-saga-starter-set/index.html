The Duke Saga Starter Set

Admin 2012/02/27

A few of our recent posts relate to the Duke trial saga. For those that want to learn more, Baggerly and Coombes have put together a “starter set”. It includes

  1. a video of one of their talks

  2. the 60 Minutes episode and clip

  3. slides from a recent presentation with some new details

  4. their  Annals of Applied Statistics paper

5. a editorial they wrote for Clinical Chemistry about what information should be required to support  clinical “omics” publications (gated)

6. links to the IOM session recordings and slides

Enjoy!
